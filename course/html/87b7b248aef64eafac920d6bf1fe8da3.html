Top tips from Dustin

  • You only get one shot - you can't ask people again
  • Pilot, pilot, pilot - others may spot something you didn't
  • Keep the survey short - longer than 10 minutes, people get bored and the quality of data reduces
  • Keep the sections short - minimise the number of questions on a page
  • Keep the questions short - consider branching questions for multiple options
  • Check out the user experience on a mobile phone
  • Only collect data you are willing to analyse - why ask a question if you're not interested in the insights?!
