Things to remember

Remember the first rule of jump_ids. If you use an iframe embed of a webpage link that is acting like a jump_id remember every time you relaunch the course you will need to manually update these links as they will not automatically populate with new course references. iFrames remain constant across courses.

Test, test, test. When you want to set up a new update with glitter and whizzy stuff, you will need to test this more than an average course content page, predominately due to the different page width. Having a few test accounts to enroll when you wish to view the new update will get around the 3-week review window (as it is highly likely you will have been enrolled on your course longer than a 3-week period by this point!)
