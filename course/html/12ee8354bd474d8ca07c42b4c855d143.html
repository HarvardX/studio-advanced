More than enough (for now)

Some of these examples are pretty extreme but they serve to highlight some radical changes that can be made, often unintentionally when focussing on something else. They also show that some changes are overridden by edX's native style sheet. When you use CSS, it can have unintended consequences when applied on page, especially on a platform that has quite a lot of its own formatting and style elements.
