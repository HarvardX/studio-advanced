Setting up cohorts


Easy

Hours

Creating cohorts can provide interesting ways to present different content to different user groups and improve user experience. However, they shouldn’t be over-applied - thinking about why you need cohorts on your course and what you hope to achieve with them is the most important first step. This is often referred to as a ‘Cohort Strategy’, and without one you might end up with an overly complex course structure that’s difficult to maintain or work out who can see what!

The goal: Split your learners into cohorots, whether to provide differentiated content or for other purposes
Prerequisites: Familiarity with edX Studio and LMS
Project length: 60 minutes to learn, 30 minutes to create strategy, 10 minutes to implement, 10 minutes to test
Ingredients: Patience - planning takes the longest time but is important to get right
