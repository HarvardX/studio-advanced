Identify a video, 5-10 seconds in length, to play before course videos. Enter the video ID from the Video Uploads page and one or more transcript files in the following format: {"video_id": "ID", "transcripts": {"language": "/static/filename.srt"}}. For example, an entry for a video with two transcripts looks like this: {"video_id": "77cef264-d6f5-4cf2-ad9d-0178ab8c77be", "transcripts": {"en": "/static/DemoX-D01_1.srt", "uk": "/static/DemoX-D01_1_uk.srt"}}

There are two things people typically do with this: put an advert before their videos, or add a "bumper" to identify their institution.
