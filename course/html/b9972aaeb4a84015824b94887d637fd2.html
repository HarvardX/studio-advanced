Enter the name of the course as it should appear in the edX.org course list.

Why do we have a star on this one? Because we want you to remember that it's ok to change your course name. As long as no one says the course name in a video, you can listen to your marketing research and switch up the name to whatever you want.

Also: if you start your course by importing a pre-made template like we do, you'll end up with a course name like "HarvardX Blank Template Course". Or maybe you just made a typo. Here's the setting to fix that.
