Sample Navigation Schemes

Below you'll see a handful of alternative navigation schemes. Each of them is just a mockup. They're not hooked up to anything - you can't go anywhere by clicking on them. They're just wireframes. Take some time to look at each of them, and consider:

  • Can you immediately see how a learner would use it, or does it require explanation?
  • Does its structure give you additional information beyond what's already in the course outline?
  • Does the design have inherent accessibility issues?
  • Do you like it?

Once you've give it some thought, click on the "Our Analysis" button beneath each one to see what we think and why.

It's a long page. Feel free to skip around and see what you like best.
