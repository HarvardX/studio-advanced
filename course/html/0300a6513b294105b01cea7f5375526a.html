The fine haggis pictured below is covered with a clickable image map. Click on various parts of the noble beast to learn more about it. There are also links beneath it that will open the same pop-up boxes.

The radar arrays are located at the top of the creature's head, on the left- and right-hand sides. They enable the beast to detect the sound of treats being opened up to three miles away.

The Stare Zone allows deep contemplation of the world. It is also used to indicate emotions such as hunger, comfort, and total insanity. Located in the middle of the head.

Directly beneath the stare zone is the Boop Button! This important location can be gently pressed to mildly annoy the beast.

This haggis is covered in floofs all around its body. They cover it to a depth of approximately one kilometer, providing excellent protection against deep snow.

At the end of the body is the Auxiliary Fluff, which is used for something I'm sure.
