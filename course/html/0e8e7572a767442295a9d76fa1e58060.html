Do you have loads of questions for assessments but only want to show a selection to learners? Or do you want to randomise questions to make a revision tool? Well, that is basically all you can do with content libraries! Do not be fooled, it is not a mixed media asset repository, and has a number of limitations, but if you want to use it in exactly the way it was designed, it works fine.
