EdX Advanced Settings


Easy

Hour

EdX has a number of advanced settings, most of which are not well-documented or are listed across multiple pages. Here we're collecting all of the information about the Advanced Settings page in one place.

As the warning says, "Do not modify these policies unless you are familiar with their purpose." If you're going to test out variations on these settings, have a backup of your course ready and have the Import page loaded, just in case you make Studio unusable and need to restore.

Prerequisites: None.
Project length: 20 minutes to select and change settings, 10 minutes to test.
Ingredients: None. Anyone with access to Studio can do this.
