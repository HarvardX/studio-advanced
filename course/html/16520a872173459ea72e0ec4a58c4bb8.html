When is too much?

So why are we making such a fuss about simple CSS? Take a look for yourself!

Below is a drop-down list of different elements that *could* be changed using CSS and their cataclysmic effect on the look and feel of a page when implemented. Try out the drop-down. You can press 'escape' to cancel any of the effects. 

Questions to ask yourself as you toggle between the examples:

  • What is the impact on native edX design features?
  • Is the formatting applied consistently across the whole page?
  • Does anything else change that you hadn’t anticipated?
