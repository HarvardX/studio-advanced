Demo: Single Carousel

The example below is a revolving carousel of cute animals.

This is Heimdall the cat. He can be seen in his comfy bed near the radiator, soaking up warmth.

Kymco the cat is certain that you did not catch her playing, because she is very serious and does not do that.

Rocket, a black kitten, is here pictured not playing, which is kind of strange for her.

This might look like it's Rocket, but it's actually her sister Nebula, fast asleep on a comfortable couch arm.

Greer the chihuahua is shown here in one of her favorite spots, on top of a blanket. It could be better if she were on a lap too.

This is Pippa, also known as the Haggis. She holds the record for greatest concentration of fluff in the state of Massachusetts.

Lady Greer is ready for her close-up now. Being under a blanket is almost as good as being both under and on top of it.
