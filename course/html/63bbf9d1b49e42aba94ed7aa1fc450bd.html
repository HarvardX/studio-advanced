Docs and Sample Links

Here's the official edX documentation for this feature. It includes most of the information you'd need, but here are a few extra examples of tricks you can pull:

  • Linking to verticals
    https://courses.edx.org/xblock/block-v1:HarvardX+StudioAdv1+2T2019+type@vertical+block@c14dedcf9bf34effb28e2242675f6053
  • Linking to subsections
    https://courses.edx.org/xblock/block-v1:HarvardX+StudioAdv1+2T2019+type@sequential+block@8e194ecfdaee45a1a374ca45dd70884f
    See the navigation at the top? This is the only page in this subsection, so there's only one tab.
  • Linking to entire sections
    https://courses.edx.org/xblock/block-v1:HarvardX+StudioAdv1+2T2019+type@chapter+block@57d4f10086ac4e3ea64ff76aba7b26ae
    Note the weird double-navigation at the top.
  • Linking to Studio!
    https://studio.edx.org/container/block-v1:HarvardX+StudioAdv1+2T2019+type@html+block@9525d81a151d4da6ba3ca19015631db4
    (You can't actually see this, because you're not on the course staff for this class, but it works. Try adapting the URL to pull up something from your own courses.)
