A Sample Content Experiment

Below you'll see a very simple content experiment. You will be in one of three groups: A, B, or C.

  • Group A will see a multiple-choice problem.
  • Group B will see a check-all-that-apply problem.
  • Group C will see a dropdown problem.

All three problems have the exact same choices, with the same wording and the same answer. Only the format of the problem differs.

No choice in this adventure

There's no way for your learners to switch between content groups, so this is not a way to implement a choose-your-own adventure. They get what they get.
