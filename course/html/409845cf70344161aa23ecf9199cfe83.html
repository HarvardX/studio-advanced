International Settings

Default: null (but actually Mon DD, YYYY)

Other settings: See below.

Enter the format for due dates. The default is Mon DD, YYYY. Enter "%m-%d-%Y" for MM-DD-YYYY, "%d-%m-%Y" for DD-MM-YYYY, "%Y-%m-%d" for YYYY-MM-DD, or "%Y-%d-%m" for YYYY-DD-MM.

Nothing special for us to say here, we just wanted to star this because international learners can get confused between the US month-first and the rest-of-the-world day-first conventions. Be explicit with your learners and tell them what format you are using.
