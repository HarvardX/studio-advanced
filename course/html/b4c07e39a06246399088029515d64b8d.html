Open Response Assessments


Easy

Hour

They're not fast or easy to make, they're a little clunky and tedious for the learners, and you don't want to overuse them, but Open Response Assessment can do so much more than just "peer-graded essay questions". Let's take a look.

Prerequisites: None.
Project length: 60 minutes to learn, 30-60 minutes to build, 30-60 minutes to test.
Ingredients: None. All you need is a working edX Studio.
