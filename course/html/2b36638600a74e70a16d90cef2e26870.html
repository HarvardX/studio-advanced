If no one is engaging with the interactive component, no matter how long it took you to build, it is worth reconsidering the appropriateness of the asset.

Top tip: never put more than one interactive component per page, especially if you want learners to engage with it fully. If it's a particularly meaty activity, maybe give it its own page - this will nudge learners to pay more attention to your design decision, as they haven't been bombarded with content before and after.
