Linking your Videos


Easy

Hour

HTML

Some video-hosting platforms allow you to pop up little links within your video, for extra info or advertising or what-have-you. Now you can do that too! We'll show you how.

This project is in the "Operating Procedures" section because to really use it well, you want to use it throughout your course and not just once. That means adding an extra step to your video process, around the same time that you'd be inserting images or stock footage. You can try to do it all afterward, but it's going to feel like a much bigger lift then. Make it a regular part of your practice and it'll be much easier on you.

Check out the demo on the next page.

The goal: Pop up links during your videos.
Prerequisites: Familiarity with HTML.
Project length: 1 hour to implement.
Ingredients:

  • A video.
  • Access to Studio.
  • Several files from the HX-JS repository will be needed. You can get these by downloading individual scripts, or by cloning or downloading the whole repository.
