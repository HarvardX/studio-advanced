Did you know it's possible to add more to the home page than just a plain text announcement?

Did you know it's possible to add content such as images, videos and even reference your course CSS for branding consistency?

Did you know you can add glitter too?!

Well, the glitter is maybe pushing it but everything else is true! Your front page can celebrate all the beauty of your other course pages to welcome your new learners into a truly exciting learning experience. So what are you waiting for, bring on the shiny stuff!

The goal: Make a great first impression by showing something different to learners
Prerequisites: CSS entry-level skillz (if using)
Project length: 30 minutes to learn, 1 hour to implement, 30 minutes to test
Ingredients: A vision for beauty (or at least an idea of intended user experience)
