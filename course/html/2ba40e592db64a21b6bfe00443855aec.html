Aren't basic questions boring?

Simple answer, no.

We are all familiar with basic question types. These are the most common forms of assessment on almost every learning platform. Usually, this is where you pose a question to the learner and provide them pre-set answers to select from - traditional MCQ style. Numerical problems and check-all-that-apply problems fit into this box too. You might have already experimented with open text, short answers - requiring the learner to submit a specific word or numeral without explicit answers to select from - but it is possible to do a lot more with basic question types.
