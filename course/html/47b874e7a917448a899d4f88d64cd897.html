Survey support - getting started

When it comes to setting up a survey, especially if you are planning to use the data for research, putting aside more time than you initially think is certainly recommended. (If you need to go through ethics approval boards, probably double your estimate again!)

Most people have done surveys before and some people specialise, conducting research through surveys regularly. Whatever your experience level, you've probably got some handy tips to share and we'd love to hear from you. You can never have too much advice when planning surveys!

However, to get you started, we've brought together some top tips from those who lead on survey planning and setup across a number of different organisations to help you leap-frog in your own setup.
