Linking to components with XBlock URLs

XBLock URLs are a way to pull a single component from a course. For instance, here's a link to this very component itself.

What can you do with this?

  • You can iframe one part of a course into another place in that course, like we did with the intro survey in this course.
  • You can iframe one part of a course into a different course entirely - for instance, if you wanted to draw connections for learners who are signed up for both courses.
  • You can build apps that deliver an edX course in a different way. The edX mobile app itself works via XBlock URL.

You cannot use XBlock URLs to show components to people who aren't logged into the course. XBlock URLs are aware of most of edX's restrictions, like login status, user type, cohort, etc.
