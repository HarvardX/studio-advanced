Image Carousel


Easy

Hour

HTML

Image carousels, also called "sliders," are a great way to present several images in a way that people can navigate and refer back to without filling up a ton of vertical space on the page. We're using the Slick slider as the base for ours - it's highly customizable and looks like it belongs in the edX platform.

The goal: Build a carousel of images or text blocks.
Prerequisites: Familiarity with HTML.
Project length: 10 minutes to learn, 20 minutes to implement, 5 minutes to test.
Ingredients:

  • The usual set of files from the HX-JS repository (follow the implementation instructions there). Download them and put them in Files & Uploads.
  • Several images that you provide yourself.

Original Source: The Slick image slider, provided under the MIT license.
