Give it a go

With all of this, the most important aspect is practice. The more you experiment and troubleshoot, the easier and more intuitive it will become.

Suggested next steps
  • Create a test area in an edX course you have access to.
  • Using our example.css as a starting file, upload to the course and play around with some of the HTML.
  • Once feeling more confident, manipulate some of the variables, such as border widths, colours and padding. Upload again. What happens to the test page? Was it as you expected?
  • Take a look at the css in the HX-JS Github repository.
  • Feeling adventurous? Have a look online for other bits of simple HTML or develop your own .css file.
  • Fallen in love with CSS? Sign up to one of the many online courses that take you through more advanced features.
