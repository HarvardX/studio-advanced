Blue Boxes

Supplemental text and additional information. Probably important to read.

Purple Boxes

Questions and examples. Probably OK to skip, at least the first time through.

Red Boxes

Warnings! Pay close attention!

If you see a small icon that's not on this list, it's probably just an emoji 😃 or FontAwesome smiley icon, and it's there to brighten your day.
